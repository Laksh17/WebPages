Panache Restaurants

History

Panache was a restaurant started by Van Louie and his wife Michelle in the year 1879. With a small budget and not more than half an acre of land, he started his business with the main aim of catering to as many people as he could by satisfying their unique tastes. His restaurant soon became popular but suffered heavy losses during the world wars due to shortage of produce. It was reopened in the year 1967 with the same intention of achieveing the very aims it once had.

About


We at Panache always believe that the freshness and quality of a restaurant's vegetables can either make or break the dish served. Therefore, our chefs always make use of the most fresh produce there is to create their signanture delicacies.


We believe that our dairy is worth boasting about simply because of the fact that our supply of milk comes from three of the most trusted farms in this town. We have a special class of chefs dedicated to use the milk to create other essential diary products like cheese, butter, etc,.


Another noteworthy sector of our restaurant is our baking department. Our breads contain no artificial agents and almost 90% of our breads are gluten free in order to cater to the health conscious diners. The goodness of your grandma's baking is all packed in here too :)
